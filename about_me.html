A Little About Me

Moving to Utah wasn’t all that bad after all. After a couple of
months of being homesick I quickly became an enthusiastic
of snow boarding during the cold winter days and during
summer we would take our motorcycles out for a spin and
enjoy the summer heat. What better way to warm up to a
State that isn’t home. I now love Utah and I visit my home
state once a year. I married my high school sweetheart
Lorena 11 years ago and now we have three beautiful
daughters our oldest Isabella (11), our middle child Emma
Sofia (7) and our youngest Victoria (5).

I enjoy participating in Axe Throwing Leagues and
Softball leagues or anything else that involves beer.
